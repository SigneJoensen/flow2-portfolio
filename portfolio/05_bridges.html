Bridges

Copenhagen is a city filled with bridges. Big and small, known and unknown. ‘Bridges’ is a school project which began with a photo safari around Copenhagen. The task was to find or create a story revolving one of these bridges and document it. Later, bringing the pictures into Adobe Photoshop, we had to alter them by adding and removing elements and by changing the lighting and coloring.
